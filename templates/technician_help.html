Frequently Asked Questions

{{ faqs|length if faqs else 8 }} questions
📋 How do I accept a new booking?
Go to your Dashboard and click on the pending booking. You'll see "Accept" button. Click it to confirm the job.
💰 How do I get paid?
Payments are processed weekly. You can check your earnings in the Profile section. Payment is transferred to your registered bank account every Friday.
🔄 How to update booking status?
Open the booking and click on "Update Status". You can change it to In Progress, Completed, or Reschedule as needed.
📞 Can't reach customer?
Click the "Call" button next to customer's name. If still unreachable, contact support and we'll help you reschedule.
🛠️ Need spare parts?
Check inventory in your dashboard. If part is not available, request it through "Request Parts" option.
📅 How to reschedule?
Contact the customer first, then use "Reschedule" option in the booking. New time will be confirmed via notification.
⭐ Customer feedback?
After completing a job, customers can leave feedback. Check your rating in Profile section.
🚗 Travel allowance?
Travel allowance is included in your earnings based on distance. Check your weekly payment breakdown.
